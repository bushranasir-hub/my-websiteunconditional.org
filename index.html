About
Un-conditional Inc.

Our Story

More Than a Nonprofit — A Movement.

Un-conditional Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization established in 2015 with a clear mission: provide resources to pregnant and parenting teens to help them navigate the dual journey of youth and parenthood.

We believe every young parent already has the strength within them — they simply need the right tools, community, and opportunities to bring it forward. We meet teens where they are — without judgment or barriers — so they can shape their own stories with confidence.

"We shape our own stories. We don't let circumstances write them for us."

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2015
Established
Un-conditional Inc. was formed as a nonprofit organization to provide tangible support to pregnant and parenting teens in the metro-Atlanta area.
2016 – 2019
Events & Outreach
Signature events like "Unwrap Hope for the Holidays" brought our community together and amplified our impact across the metro-Atlanta area.
2020 – 2023
H.E.L.P. Initiative
We launched the H.E.L.P. Initiative — a structured framework built on four pillars to provide holistic support for pregnant and parenting teens across the metro-Atlanta area.
2024 – 2025
Enhanced Community Partnerships
We forged collaborative partnerships with organizations like Michael A. Grant Boys & Girls Club, Cobb Collaborative, WIC-Cobb Douglas Public Health, and others to extend our reach.
2026
H.E.L.P. Summit — December 2026
Our landmark H.E.L.P. Summit is scheduled for December 5, 2026 in Marietta, GA — bringing together teen parents, sponsors, volunteers, and community leaders for a day of empowerment.

Two Promises We Keep

How We Show Up Every Day

We
We

Stand Together

No young parent should have to figure it out alone. We show up — consistently, without judgment — to walk alongside pregnant teens and young families through every challenge and milestone.

We believe every parent desires to do their best, and every child deserves the best. That belief drives everything we do.

  • Providing a non-judgmental, safe space for every young parent
  • Aligning families with community resources and local partners
  • Creating peer support networks and mentorship opportunities
  • Hosting events that bring the community together around shared goals
Build
Build

Collaborative Partnerships

We don't work in isolation — we build bridges. By connecting young families with community organizations, healthcare providers, schools, and local businesses, we multiply the support available to every family we serve.

Strong partnerships mean more resources, more reach, and more lasting impact. Together, we create the conditions for real, generational change.

  • Partnering with local health, education and social service organizations
  • Engaging corporate sponsors who believe in our mission
  • Building bridges between teen parents and essential institutions
  • Fostering long-term relationships that create lasting change

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access your services?
Our resources are available to pregnant teens and parenting teens — regardless of age, race, background, or circumstance. We provide support without conditions or judgment.
Are all resources really free?
Yes — 100%. Every resource, program, workshop, and service we offer is provided at absolutely no cost to the families we serve. This is made possible through the generosity of our sponsors, donors, and volunteers.
How can I volunteer with Un-conditional Inc.?
We welcome volunteers (18 and older) who share our passion for empowering teen families! Simply fill out the contact form above, select "I'd like to volunteer," and tell us about your skills and availability.
How can my company become a corporate sponsor?
We offer several sponsorship tiers with different levels of visibility and involvement. Visit our Sponsors section for more details, or contact us directly and we'll send you our sponsorship package.
Is Un-conditional Inc. a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit?
Yes. Un-conditional Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization • EIN: 33-2817659, which means all donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent permitted by law. We have been operating since 2015.
What areas do you serve?
We partner with local organizations throughout Georgia to serve the greater Metro Atlanta area, including Fulton, Cobb, Douglas, Paulding, Dekalb, Clayton, and surrounding counties.
